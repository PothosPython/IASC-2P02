Week 5 - Plato

Specifications

  • The scene must have a working directionalLight that casts shadows.
  • The scene must have a mesh that moves. This mesh must cast shadows.
  • The scene must have a plane that receives shadows from the directionalLight and the moving mesh.
  • The shadows from the moving mesh must appear on the plane.
  • The scene must have working OrbitControls.
